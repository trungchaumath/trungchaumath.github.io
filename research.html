I'd like to think of myself as a jack of all trades, and master of one. The only topic I can say I am an expert of is Barile-Macchia resolutions. Here are the broad topics I'm currently working on:

  • Constructing minimal free resolutions of monomial ideals
  • Persistence of associated primes of powers of monomial ideals
  • Computing regularity of powers of ideals
  • F-regularity/F-purity/F-injectivity
  • Golod rings
  • DG-algebras
  • Neural ideals
  • Chip-firing game
  • Test ideals in prime characteristic
  • Squarefree powers, symbolic powers
